Berilgan koordinatalar: K1(x1, y1, z1), K2(x2, y2, z2), K3(x3,y3,z3), K4(x4, y4, z4) Masofalar(R1, R2, R3, R4)

$$ (x^2-x1^2) + (y^2-y1^2) +(z^2-z1^2) = R1^2 .$$ $$ (x^2-x2^2) + (y^2-y2^2) +(z^2-z2^2) = R2^2 .$$ $$ (x^2-x3^2) + (y^2-y3^2) +(z^2-z3^2) = R3^2 .$$ $$ (x^2-x4^2) + (y^2-y4^2) +(z^2-z4^2) = R4^2 .$$

Tenglamalarni o'zaro ayiramiz

(2 -> 1)

$$ 2x*(x1-x2) + 2y*(y1-y2) +2z*(z1-z2) = R2^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x2^2)+(y1^2-y2^2)+(z1^2-z2^2) .$$

(3 -> 1)

$$ 2x*(x1-x3) + 2y*(y1-y3) +2z*(z1-z3) = R3^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x3^2)+(y1^2-y3^2)+(z1^2-z3^2) .$$

(4 -> 1)

$$ 2x*(x1-x4) + 2y*(y1-y4) +2z*(z1-z4) = R4^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x4^2)+(y1^2-y4^2)+(z1^2-z4^2) .$$

Koeffitsientlarga almashtirish kiritamiz

$$ 2*(x1-x2) = a11 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y2) = a12 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z2) = a13 .$$
$$ 2*(x1-x3) = a21 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y3) = a22 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z3) = a23 .$$
$$ 2*(x1-x4) = a31 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y4) = a32 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z4) = a33 .$$
$$ R2^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x2^2)+(y1^2-y2^2)+(z1^2-z2^2) = d1 .$$ $$ R3^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x3^2)+(y1^2-y3^2)+(z1^2-z3^2) = d2 .$$ $$ R4^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x4^2)+(y1^2-y4^2)+(z1^2-z4^2) = d3 .$$

Chiziqli 3 nomalumli tenglamalar sistemasi

$$ 1)(a11*)=> a11*x + a12*y = d1 - a13*z $$ $$ 2)(a21*)=> a21*x + a22*y = d2 - a23*z $$ $$ 1)a21*a11*x + a21*a12*y = a21*d1 - a21*a13*z $$ $$ 2)a11*a21*x + a11*a22*y = a11*d3 - a11*a23*z $$

(2 -> 1)

$$ y*(a11*a22 - a12*a21) + (a23*a11-a13*a21)*z = d2*a11 - d1*a21 $$
$$ (a11*)=> a11*x + a12*y + a13*z = d1 $$ $$ (a31*)=> a31*x + a32*y + a33*z = d3 $$ $$ a31*a11*x + a31*a12*y + a31*a13*z= a31*d1 $$ $$ a11*a31*x + a11*a32*y + a11*a33*z= a11*d3 $$

(3 -> 1)

$$ y*(a11*a32 - a12*a31) + (a33*a11-a13*a31)*z = d3*a11 - d1*a31 $$

y qiymatini z bilan bog'liq ifoda orqali topish

$$ a22*a11 - a12*a21 = b11 $$ $$ a23*a11 - a21*a13 = b12 $$ $$ a32*a11 - a31*a12 = b21 $$ $$ a33*a11 - a31*a13 = b22 $$ $$ d2*a11 - d1*a21 = k1 $$ $$ d3*a11 - d1*a31 = k2 $$

chiziqli tenglama

$$ y*b11 + z*b12 = k1 $$ $$ y*b21 + z*b22 = k2 $$
$$(b21*)-> b11*b21*y + b12*b21*z = b21*k1 $$ $$ (b11*)-> b21*b11*y + b22*b*11*z= b11*k2 $$

Tenglama hisoblanadi

$$ z = {b21*k1 - b11*k2\over b21*b12-b11*b22} $$
$$ y = { k1-z*b12\over b11} $$ $$ y = { k1-({b21*k1 - b11*k2\over b21*b12-b11*b22})*b12\over b11} $$

x qiymatini topamiz

$$ a11*x + a12*y + a13*z = d1 $$ $$ x = {d1 - a13*z - a12*y \over a11} $$ $$ x = {d1 - a13*({b21*k1 - b11*k2\over b21*b12-b11*b22}) - a12*({ k1-({b21*k1 - b11*k2\over b21*b12-b11*b22})*b12\over b11}) \over a11} $$

Berilgan koordinatalar: K1(x1, y1, z1), K2(x2, y2, z2), K3(x3,y3,z3) Masofalar(R1, R2, R3)

$$ (x^2-x1^2) + (y^2-y1^2) +(z^2-z1^2) = R1^2 .$$ $$ (x^2-x2^2) + (y^2-y2^2) +(z^2-z2^2) = R2^2 .$$ $$ (x^2-x3^2) + (y^2-y3^2) +(z^2-z3^2) = R3^2 .$$

Tenglamalarni o'zaro ayiramiz"

(2 -> 1)

$$ 2x*(x1-x2) + 2y*(y1-y2) +2z*(z1-z2) = R2^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x2^2)+(y1^2-y2^2)+(z1^2-z2^2) .$$

(3 -> 1)

$$ 2x*(x1-x3) + 2y*(y1-y3) +2z*(z1-z3) = R3^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x3^2)+(y1^2-y3^2)+(z1^2-z3^2) .$$

Koeffitsientlarga almashtirish kiritamiz

$$ 2*(x1-x2) = a11 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y2) = a12 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z2) = a13 .$$
$$ 2*(x1-x3) = a21 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y3) = a22 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z3) = a23 .$$
$$ R2^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x2^2)+(y1^2-y2^2)+(z1^2-z2^2) = d1 .$$ $$ R3^2-R1^2 + (x1^2-x3^2)+(y1^2-y3^2)+(z1^2-z3^2) = d2 .$$

Chiziqli 3 nomalumli tenglamalar sistemasi

$$ (a11*)=> a11*x + a12*y = d1 - a13*z $$ $$ (a21*)=> a21*x + a22*y = d1 - a23*z $$
$$ a21*a11*x + a21*a12*y = a21*d1 - a21*a13*z $$ $$ a11*a21*x + a11*a22*y = a11*d1 - a11*a23*z $$

y qiymatini z bilan bog'liq ifoda orqali topish

$$ y*(a12*a21-a22*a11) = a21*d1 - a11*d2 + (a23*a11 - a13*a21)*z $$ $$ y = {d1*a21 - d2*a11 + (a23*a11 - a13*a21)*z\over a12*a21 - a22*a11} $$

=>

$$ a12*a21 - a22*a11 = b1 $$
$$ a23*a11 - a13*a21 = b2 $$
$$ d1*a21 - d2*a11 = t11 $$

$$ y = {t11 + b2*z \over b1} $$

x ni z orqali ifodalaymiz

$$ a11*x = d1 - a12*y - a13*z $$ $$ x = { d1 - a12*y - a13*z\over a11} $$
$$ x = { d1 - a12*({t11 + b2*z \over b1}) - a13*z\over a11} $$ $$ x = { {d1\over a11} - { a12*t11 + (b2*a12 + a13*b1)*z\over a11*b1}} $$

x qiymatini topamiz

$$ x = { d1*b1 - a12*t11 - (b2*a12 + a13*b1)*z\over a11*b1} $$ $$ x = { (d1*b1 - a12*t11) - (b2*a12 + a13*b1)*z\over a11*b1} $$

== >

$$ d1*b1 - a12*t11 = t21 $$
$$ b2*a12 + a13*b1 = t22 $$
$$ d1*a21 - d2*a11 = t11 $$

$$ x = {t21 - t22*z \over a11*b1} $$

Kvadrat tenglama koeffitsientlari topiladi

$$ 1) ({t21-t22*z\over a11*b1} -x1)^2 + ({t11 + b2*z\over b1} - y1)^2 + (z-z1)^2 = R1^2 $$ $$ 2) ({t21-a11*b1*x1\over a11*b1} - {t22*z\over a11*b1})^2 + ({t11 - b1*y1\over b1} - {b2*z\over b1})^2 + (z-z1)^2 = R1^2 $$ $$ 3) ({t21-a11*b1*x1\over a11*b1})^2 - 2*({t21-a11*b1*x1\over a11*b1})*{t22*z\over a11*b1}) + ({t22*z\over a11*b1})^2 + ({t11 - b1*y1\over b1})^2 - 2*{t11 - b1*y1\over b1}*{b2*z\over b1}) + {b2*z\over b1}^2 + z^2-2*z1*z+z1^2 = R1^2 $$

kvadrat tenglama koeffitsientlari topiladi

$$ 1) ({t22*z\over a11*b1})^2 + {b2*z\over b1}^2 + z^2 = k1 $$
$$ 2) - 2*({t21-a11*b1*x1\over a11*b1})*{t22*z\over a11*b1}) + 2*{t11 - b1*y1\over b1}*{b2*z\over b1}) -2*z1*z = k2 $$
$$ 3) ({t21-a11*b1*x1\over a11*b1})^2 + ({t11 - b1*y1\over b1})^2 + z1^2 - R1^2 = k3 $$

Kvadrat tenglamadan x, y, z topiladi

$$ k1*z^2 + k2*z + k3 = 0 $$
$$ z = {-k2 + \sqrt[2]{k2^2-4*k1*k3}\over 2*k1}$$
$$ x = { t21 - t22*({-k2 + \sqrt[2]{k2^2-4*k1*k3}\over 2*k1})\over a11*b1}$$
$$ y = {t11 + b2*({-k2 + \sqrt[2]{k2^2-4*k1*k3}\over 2*k1})\over b1} $$
Xatolikni hisoblash
$$ 2*(x1-x2) = a11 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y2) = a12 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z2) = a13 .$$
$$ 2*(x1-x3) = a21 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y3) = a22 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z3) = a23 .$$
$$ 2*(x1-x4) = a31 .$$ $$ 2*(y1-y4) = a32 .$$ $$ 2*(z1-z4) = a33 .$$
$$\color{navy} (a11)*x + (a12)*y +(a13)*z = d1 + \triangle d1 $$ $$\color{navy} (a21)*x + (a22)*y +(a23)*z = d2 + \triangle d2 $$ $$\color{navy} (a31)*x + (a32)*y +(a33)*z = d3 + \triangle d3 $$

$$ \left(\begin{array}{c}\triangle d1\\ \triangle d2 \\ \triangle d3 \end{array}\right) => \triangle dx $$

$$ \bf A = \begin{bmatrix}a11 & a12 & a13 \\ a21 & a22 & a23 \\ a31 & a32 & a33 \end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{navy} {\bf det(A)} = (a11*a22*a33 + a13*a21*a32 + a12*a23*a31) - (a13*a22*a31+a11*a23*a32+a12*a21*a33)$$

$$\color{olive} \bf A12 = -\begin{bmatrix} a21 & a23 \\ a31 & a33\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{olive} \bf A13 = \begin{bmatrix} a21 & a22 \\ a31 & a32\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{navy} \bf A21 = -\begin{bmatrix} a12 & a13 \\ a32 & a33\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{navy} \bf A22 = \begin{bmatrix} a11 & a13 \\ a31 & a33\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{navy} \bf A23 = -\begin{bmatrix} a11 & a12 \\ a31 & a32\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{green} \bf A31 = \begin{bmatrix} a12 & a13 \\ a22 & a23\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{green} \bf A32 = -\begin{bmatrix} a11 & a13 \\ a21 & a23\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\color{green} \bf A33 = \begin{bmatrix} a11 & a12 \\ a21 & a22\end{bmatrix} $$
